Queue Orchestrator Visualization

Interactive demonstration of task queue management

How it works

This visualization demonstrates how an orchestrator manages tasks with specific rules:
  • EOS requests are given highest priority in the queue
  • Tasks are processed based on both priority and arrival time
  • Duplicate entries are handled by removing the oldest occurrence
  • In-progress tasks with duplicates remain pending until completion
  • Maximum parallel task execution is limited to 3
